How we work

Three tenets,
tested on every site.

The operating principles behind how we hire, deploy, and partner. They've been hard-won — from running networks across the continent and shipping infrastructure into places everyone else said couldn't take it.

01 / PRESENCE

We build where the work is.

On-the-ground operators in every region we serve. Engineers who know which substation matters. No air-freighted spares from Frankfurt forever.

02 / INTEGRATION

Vertical stack, horizontal partners.

The stack is ours, end-to-end. The relationships around it — equipment, capital, universities, governments — are earned and reciprocal.

03 / CAPACITY

We hire for the next site.

Always slightly ahead of demand, never behind. Onboarding compounds; the second site is faster than the first because the second hire was already in seat.

How we hire

If you can build it,
we want to talk.

We're hiring across infrastructure engineering, cloud operations, cybersecurity, business development, and field engineering. Most roles are based at Hells Gate (Naivasha), Nairobi, or remote with travel.

Internships at Hells Gate run year-round for university partners. Email careers@navonworld.com with a one-page brief and we'll respond within five working days.
